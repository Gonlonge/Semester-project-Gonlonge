The Science Museum of Norway

Our goal as a museum is to create a space where everyone can experience the wonders of our discoveries. Are you looking to get involved with our team of researchers and academics? We offer ways for you to use our resources to be able to contribute.

For Kids

Are you a young one looking to learn more about science? We have the Young Stars club which meets once a week on Saturdays between 10:00 and 13:00 where you’ll get to explore and experiment with our team of experts.

Biology and Nano Robotics

From micro-organisms to human anatomy, major breakthroughs in biology offer us unique insights into the great wonders of the world. Learn how life expectancy expanded and the quality of life improved with us.

Restrictions

Please stay at home if you experience any symptoms related to Covid-19. We take it very seriously and keeping our guests safe is a main priority for us. There are antibacterial stations in each room. Kindly use a facemask and keep your distance to other visitors that are not part of your group.
