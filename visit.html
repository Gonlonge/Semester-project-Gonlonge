The Science Museum of Norway

Our museum is to create a space where everyone can experience the wonders of our discoveries. Are you looking to get involved with our team of researchers and academics? Our museum offers various ways for you to use our resources and contribute.

For Kids

Are you a young person looking to learn more about science? We have the Young Stars club which meets once a week on a Saturday between 10:00 and 13:00 where you’ll get to explore and experiment with our team of experts.

Biology and Nano Robotics

From micro-organisms to the human body, major breakthroughs in biology are offering us unique insights into the great wonders of the tiny world. For many people their life expectancy is much longer and their quality of life much improved
