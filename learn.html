Learn More about Science

Are you looking to get involved with our team of researchers and academics? Our museum offers various ways for you to use our resources and contribute towards them. We have online recordings, laboratory space, and working relations with a number of universities across the country.

For teachers

As a community-driven museum, we want to work with schools to create a space for learning and exploring. Our team are on hand to give your students guided tours and teachings in the learning laboratory. We provide great video presentations that excite and inspire, do not hesitate to get in touch to find out more about our opportunities or any related questions.
